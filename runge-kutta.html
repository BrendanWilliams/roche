Gravitational acceleration

\(x\) is a position vector here. $$ f(x) = \frac{x}{|x|^3} $$

Euler method

$$ v(t+h) = v(t) + h f(x) \\ x(t+h) = x(t) + h v(t) $$

Modified Euler method (Second-order Runge Kutta method)

$$ v_1 = v(t) + h f(x) \\ x_1 = x(t) + h v_1(t) \\ v_2 = v_1 + h f(x_1) \\ x_2 = x_1 + h v_2 \\ v(t+h) = \frac{v_1 + v_2}{2} \\ x(t+h) = \frac{x_1 + x_2}{2} $$
